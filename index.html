After the remarkable success of mesmerizing intra university Brand Master 16 and inter university Brand Master17, UIU Career Counselling Centre along with UIU MARKETING FORUM proudly presents “BRAND MASTER 2018– Know Me by Name”. “Brand master 2018” is an INTER-UNIVERSITY Branding competition where, firstly, the students can exhibit their own PROSPECTIVE TALENTS of branding of any given product in the workshop, secondly, students can develop branding & marketing skills through this competition.
